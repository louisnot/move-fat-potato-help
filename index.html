Quick Guide

Welcome! This guide explains how to get started and use the main features of the app.

1. Create your account

• Open the app
• Sign up with a username
• Accept the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy

2. Connect Apple Health

• Tap Continue when asked to connect Apple Health
• Allow the app to read your step count
• The app only reads daily steps to power rankings

The app does not access other health information such as heart rate, workouts, or medical data.

3. Track your progress

• Your daily steps will automatically sync
• Watch your progress grow over time
• Compare your steps with friends on the leaderboard

4. Leaderboards

• Compete with friends
• See who walks the most
• Try to reach the top of the leaderboard

5. Managing your data

• You can delete your account anytime in the app settings
• You can revoke Health access in iOS settings

To manage Health permissions go to:
iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Your App
